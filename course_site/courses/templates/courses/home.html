The app allows coordinators to create and manage courses with interactive chapters and questions. Users can engage with course content and receive instant feedback. Staff and users can communicate securely via private messaging, with staff able to manage and reply to user messages. The platform features role-based access, custom authentication, and a user-friendly interface for seamless interaction.
